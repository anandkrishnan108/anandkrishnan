Soccer


Soccer is my favorite sport, pastime, and stress reliever. I kicked a ball around since I was two, joined my first team when I was six, and watched the legendary Manchester United take on brilliant Barcelona one summer day in 2011 in the UEFA Champions League Final at the historic Wembley Stadium. Barcelona won 3-1, completely dominating United, and the sight of Perdo Rodriguez, David Villa, and the sensational Lionel Messi scoring in the final lit my world up. The beautiful game had stolen my heart.

I have been playing soccer for 11 years now. I played all four years in high school, and had the honor of being a Hawks Varsity soccer player (jersey #18).

My most memorable moment of the season was when I scored my first goal for the program, against our rivals Schaumburg no less. It was 0-0 with 5 minutes left in the first half when we got a corner kick. I was waiting outside of the box for a rebound. My teammate took the corner, and the Schaumburg defense cleared it out to me. When the ball got to me, even though it was bouncing and I was 25 yards out, I decided to hit it first time to catch the goalie off guard. Also, since I play defense, I almost never get to shoot, so I thought "why not". I connected with the ball really cleanly, and time literally slowed for me as everyone in the field and stands watched the ball perfectly curl into the top right corner. We ended up dominating the rest of the game and winning 4-0.
